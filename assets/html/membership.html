Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join JPCS?

JPCS is open to all students enrolled in computer science, information technology, information systems, and related programs. We also welcome students from other disciplines who are passionate about technology.

What are the membership fees used for?

Membership fees help fund workshops, hackathons, guest speaker events, equipment, and other resources for our members. We maintain financial transparency and provide annual reports to our members.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes! You can upgrade your membership at any time. The remaining value of your current membership will be applied towards the upgrade cost.

How long does the application process take?

Applications are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Once approved, you'll receive a welcome email with instructions on how to access member resources.

What happens after I submit my application?

You'll receive a confirmation email with your application details. Our membership committee will review your application, and you'll be notified of the status via email within 3-5 business days.

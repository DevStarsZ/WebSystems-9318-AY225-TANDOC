About JPCS

Empowering the next generation of technology leaders since 1987

Our Mission

To develop competent and socially responsible computer science professionals who will contribute to nation-building through technological innovation and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization in the Philippines that nurtures future-ready tech leaders and innovators who shape the digital future.

Our Journey

1987

Foundation

JPCS was established as the official student organization for Computer Science students nationwide.

1995

First National Convention

Hosted the inaugural national convention with over 500 participants from different universities.

2005

Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with major tech companies for internships and research collaborations.

2018

Digital Transformation

Launched digital platforms and expanded online learning resources for members.

2024

Future Ready

Focusing on AI, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies to prepare members for Industry 5.0.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during office hours. Urgent matters are prioritized.

Can I schedule a meeting with JPCS officers?

Yes! Please mention your request in the message, and we'll coordinate a suitable time.

Do you offer internships or job placements?

While we don't directly place students, we partner with companies and share opportunities through our network.

How can I sponsor or partner with JPCS?

We welcome partnerships! Select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form or email partnerships@jpcs.org.

Are there volunteer opportunities?

Yes! We're always looking for passionate volunteers. Contact us or attend our next general assembly.

Can alumni join JPCS?

Absolutely! Alumni can join as mentors, speakers, or supporting members. Contact us for alumni options.
